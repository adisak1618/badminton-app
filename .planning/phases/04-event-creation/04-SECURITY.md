## Accepted Risks

| Threat ID | Category | Rationale | Owner | Review Date |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------|-------------|
| T-04-03 | Information Disclosure — clubId in LIFF URL | clubId is a UUID (non-guessable, 122 bits of entropy). Exposure in URL is low-sensitivity: the LIFF page and all downstream API calls enforce server-side requireClubRole before returning any data or performing mutations. No club PII is exposed by the UUID alone. | Engineering | 2026-10-08 |
| T-04-09 | Denial of Service — LINE pushMessage quota | LINE free tier limits to 500 push messages/month. A flood of event creations could exhaust the quota, but (a) only admin/owner role can create events, reducing the attack surface to trusted users, and (b) pushMessage failure is caught and logged; the event record is still saved and the club is not left in a broken state. Risk is proportional to club scale. | Engineering | 2026-10-08 |
